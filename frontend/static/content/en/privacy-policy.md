**Last updated: January 2026**

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the data you explicitly provide to us when you upload your activity files (.fit files) and the associated metadata (e.g., fatigue levels, weather conditions). This includes:

- Account information (email, height, weight, etc.)
- Activity data (GPS, heart rate, pace, cadence, etc.)
- Subjective feelings and environmental observations

## 2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used for the Trail Fit Research Project to understand the physiological demands of trail running. We may aggregate your data with other users to publish scientific findings.

## 3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure, or destruction of data.

## 4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at research@trailfit.io.
